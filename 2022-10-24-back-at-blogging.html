Back at blogging in 2022

Fábio Madeira

24 October 2022 • 1 min read

After a few attempts at blogging over the last decade or so, it is the time to get back at it again!

I was trying to remember when I started blogging, and I believe it was in around 2008. I started blogging on Tumblr about things that were interesting to me at the time. Music and such things, but unfortunately those posts are forever lost. I can’t find any of my login details nor can I even recall the blog’s name!

Around 2011, I then move to write on Blogger about topics related to what I was studying and my first encounters with programming. I started on Wordpress a few times, and then found about Jekyll and GitHub Pages. The very same blogging platform I am still using to host this newer iteration of my homepage.

No big promises, but I hope this time around I can get things rolling. I am keeping older posts here for archival purposes- with the tag Archived. Feel free to have a look around!
